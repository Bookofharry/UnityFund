Status: Draft
Version: 0.1
Project: UnityFund
Team: Zero Downtime

---

# Purpose

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of UnityFund beyond the initial MVP.

It serves as a strategic guide for future development while ensuring the product remains focused on its mission of simplifying financial operations for member-based organizations.

The roadmap is directional and may evolve based on customer feedback, market needs, and technical discoveries.

---

# Product Vision

Build the leading financial operating platform for member-based organizations across Africa.

---

# Phase 1 — Hackathon MVP

Goal:

Validate the core concept and demonstrate deep integration with Nomba.

Features:

* User Authentication
* Organization Management
* Member Management
* Fund Management
* Fund Rules
* Collection Cycles
* Nomba Direct Debit
* Checkout Integration
* Virtual Accounts
* Webhook Reconciliation
* Basic Dashboard
* Basic Reports

Success Outcome:

A complete end-to-end demonstration showing how organizations can automate recurring fund collections and reconciliation.

---

# Phase 2 — Product MVP

Goal:

Prepare UnityFund for pilot organizations.

Additional Features:

* Rotational Savings automation
* Emergency Fund workflows
* Welfare Fund workflows
* Notification Center
* Approval workflows
* Improved reporting
* Activity timeline
* Audit log improvements

Success Outcome:

Organizations can actively manage recurring funds with minimal manual administration.

---

# Phase 3 — Operational Platform

Goal:

Expand UnityFund into a comprehensive operational platform.

Features:

* Loan Management
* Dividend Distribution
* Expense Tracking
* Financial Statements
* Budget Planning
* Advanced Role Permissions
* Multi-organization administration

Success Outcome:

Organizations can operate most of their recurring financial processes from UnityFund.

---

# Phase 4 — Enterprise Features

Goal:

Support larger organizations and enterprise customers.

Features:

* Multi-branch organizations
* Advanced approval chains
* Custom workflows
* Accounting integrations
* ERP integrations
* Advanced analytics
* Bulk operations

Success Outcome:

UnityFund becomes suitable for large associations and cooperatives.

---

# Phase 5 — Platform Expansion

Goal:

Grow UnityFund into a financial infrastructure platform.

Potential Features:

* Public APIs
* Third-party integrations
* Mobile applications
* AI-powered insights
* Financial forecasting
* Document management
* Marketplace integrations

Success Outcome:

UnityFund becomes the preferred financial operations platform for member-based organizations across Africa.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every future feature should:

* Solve a real organizational problem.
* Align with the product vision.
* Strengthen financial operations.
* Improve automation.
* Preserve simplicity.
* Maintain security and transparency.

Features that do not contribute to these goals should not be prioritized.

---

# Roadmap Review

The roadmap should be reviewed after:

* Customer feedback
* Pilot deployments
* Major product releases
* Significant market or regulatory changes

The roadmap is a living document and should evolve with the product.
